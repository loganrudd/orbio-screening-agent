# Restaurant FAQ (bonus RAG knowledge base)

Small curated knowledge base the agent can answer candidate questions from mid-interview
(bonus RAG feature). Keep it tiny.

## Pay & schedule
- Pay is hourly, discussed after the screening with the hiring manager.
- Shifts are scheduled weekly; the screening captures general availability only.

## Locations
- The chain has multiple locations; candidates can state a location preference.

## Process
- This screening is a first-pass conversation; a human reviewer follows up.
